### **Breast Cancer Gene Analysis – Research Project**

This research project focuses on analyzing **breast cancer gene expression data** to understand patterns related to the disease. The goal is to study how different genes behave and help in identifying important features for classification.

I worked with senior team members to perform **basic data analysis and literature review**. This project helped me understand how data science is used in **healthcare and medical research**.
Gained practical experience in applying machine learning models and training datasets for classification tasks. Assisted in data preprocessing, analysis, and literature review to understand gene expression patterns and their role in disease prediction.
